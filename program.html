Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Our flagship 3-year BCA program is built to prepare confident, career-ready graduates with strong fundamentals, practical exposure, and industry-relevant skills from day one.

Flagship Program

Bachelor of Computer
Applications (BCA)

SBG BCA College offers a single, focused program designed for students who want to build a strong future in technology. The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) combines foundational computing knowledge, modern software skills, and hands-on project experience.

Affiliated to Rani Channamma University, Belagavi, this program is structured to develop problem-solving ability, technical confidence, and job readiness through practical labs, guided projects, and continuous mentoring.

Joining Us

Eligibility & Admission

Eligibility Criteria

Passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board — any stream (Science, Commerce, Arts)
Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC category — as per university norms)
Mathematics as a subject in 10th or 12th standard is preferred but not mandatory
Must have passed English as a subject at 10th level

Admission Process

1

Fill the Application Form

Apply online or collect the form from the college office.

2

Submit Required Documents

10th & 12th marksheets, transfer certificate, ID proof, and passport photographs.

3

Merit-Based Selection

Admission offered based on 12th percentage — no entrance exam required.

4

Pay Fees & Confirm Seat

Complete fee payment and collect your provisional admission letter.
