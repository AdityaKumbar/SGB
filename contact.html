Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process?

Admissions are merit-based. Fill out the application form, submit your 10+2 marksheets and documents, and receive your admission confirmation within 3–5 working days.

When do applications open for 2025–26?

Applications for the 2025–26 academic batch are open from June 2025. Early applicants get priority consideration for merit scholarships.

Is there a campus visit facility?

Yes! We welcome prospective students and parents for campus visits every weekday. Contact us to schedule a personal tour with a faculty guide.

Are scholarships available?

Merit-based scholarships are available for students scoring above 85% in Class 12. Government SC/ST/OBC scholarships are also facilitated through the college.
