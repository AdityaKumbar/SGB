Our Management

Meet the visionary leaders and dedicated management team behind SBG BCA College — committed to delivering excellence, fostering innovation, and shaping the future of technology education in Karnataka.

Senior Leadership

The Founders & Directors

Guided by experience, driven by purpose — our founding leadership has built SBG BCA College from the ground up into a respected institution of learning.

Chairman's Photo
Chairman & Founder
Shri. Suresh B. Gowda
Chairman & Founder, SBG Groups

With over three decades of experience in education and business, Shri. Suresh B. Gowda founded SBG Groups with a singular mission — to make quality higher education accessible to every deserving student in Karnataka. His visionary leadership has transformed SBG BCA College into a benchmark for academic excellence in computer applications.

Principal's Photo
Principal
Dr. Priya R. Kulkarni
Principal & Academic Director

A PhD in Computer Science with 22 years of academic and research experience, Dr. Priya R. Kulkarni leads the college's academic strategy, curriculum development, and faculty excellence initiatives. Under her leadership, the college has achieved a 95% placement record and forged partnerships with leading technology companies across India.
