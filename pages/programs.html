Programs Offered

A single, focused 3-year undergraduate program designed to build the next generation of technology professionals — with depth, hands-on learning, and an uncompromising focus on real-world readiness.

Flagship Program

Bachelor of Computer
Applications (BCA)

The BCA program at SBG BCA College is a rigorous, 3-year undergraduate degree that covers the complete spectrum of modern computing — from programming fundamentals and data structures to cloud architecture and artificial intelligence. Affiliated to Rani Channamma University, Belagavi, the degree is recognized nationally and accepted for MCA, MBA, and other postgraduate admissions.

Our curriculum is reviewed annually in consultation with industry partners to ensure students learn technologies that are actually in demand — not outdated textbook theory. Every semester combines theoretical coursework with practical lab sessions, projects, and industry exposure.

Specialization Tracks

In the 6th Semester, students choose one specialization track aligned to their career goals — gaining depth in the area that excites them most.

Full Stack Development

Build complete web applications from front-end interfaces to back-end APIs and databases. Covers React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, REST APIs, and deployment.

React.js Node.js MongoDB REST APIs Docker

Data Science & AI

Learn to extract insights from data and build intelligent systems. Covers machine learning, neural networks, data visualization, and Python-based AI frameworks.

Python TensorFlow Pandas Machine Learning NLP

Cybersecurity

Understand and defend digital systems against threats. Covers ethical hacking, network security, cryptography, penetration testing, and security compliance.

Ethical Hacking Cryptography Firewalls Pen Testing VAPT

Cloud Computing

Joining Us

Eligibility & Admission

Eligibility Criteria

Passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board — any stream (Science, Commerce, Arts)
Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC category — as per university norms)
Mathematics as a subject in 10th or 12th standard is preferred but not mandatory
Must have passed English as a subject at 10th level

Admission Process

1

Fill the Application Form

Apply online or collect the form from the college office.

2

Submit Required Documents

10th & 12th marksheets, transfer certificate, ID proof, and passport photographs.

3

Merit-Based Selection

Admission offered based on 12th percentage — no entrance exam required.

4

Pay Fees & Confirm Seat

Complete fee payment and collect your provisional admission letter.
