Who We Are

A Legacy of Academic
Excellence & Innovation

SBG BCA College, established under the prestigious SBG Groups, has been a beacon of quality education in Computer Applications for over 15 years. Located in Belagavi, Karnataka, we are proudly affiliated to Rani Channamma University and recognized by the Government of Karnataka.

Our 3-year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program is designed to equip students with both theoretical foundations and practical industry skills — from programming and databases to full-stack development and artificial intelligence.

With a placement record of over 95% and partnerships with 50+ industry recruiters, SBG BCA College has consistently delivered graduates who are ready for the real world.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Guided by a clear purpose — to empower every student with knowledge, skills, and values that lead to meaningful careers and responsible citizenship.

Our Vision

To Be a Centre of Excellence in Technology Education

To be a nationally recognized centre of excellence that empowers students with cutting-edge technological knowledge, ethical values, and a spirit of innovation — producing graduates who drive progress in the digital economy and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

Delivering Quality Education That Creates Real Impact

Our mission is to deliver a rigorous, industry-aligned BCA curriculum that combines theoretical depth with practical experience. We are committed to fostering an inclusive learning environment, investing in modern infrastructure, building industry partnerships, and ensuring every graduate is career-ready and globally competitive.

Why Choose Us

Key Highlights

What sets SBG BCA College apart — a combination of experienced faculty, modern facilities, and a proven placement record.

Experienced Faculty

Learn from academicians and industry professionals with decades of combined experience in software development, data science, networking, and computer science research.

Modern Labs & Infrastructure

Four fully equipped computer laboratories with 200+ workstations, high-speed fibre internet, and licensed software tools — available 24/7 for student use.

Placement Support

A dedicated Training & Placement cell with partnerships across 50+ companies — including TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and regional tech firms — ensuring opportunity for every graduate.

Industry Exposure

Regular guest lectures, industry workshops, hackathons, and internship programmes ensure students experience real-world technology environments before they graduate.

What We Stand For
