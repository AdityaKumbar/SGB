Our Infrastructure

Campus Facilities

Every facility at SBG BCA College is designed with one goal — to give students the best possible environment to learn, grow, and succeed.

Computer Laboratories

Four fully equipped labs with 200+ high-performance workstations running the latest hardware. Equipped with fiber-optic internet, licensed development software (Visual Studio, IntelliJ, Eclipse, MATLAB), and available 24/7 for student use.

200+ Workstations Fibre Internet 24/7 Access

Smart Library

A completely digitized library with over 10,000 books, e-journals, and research databases. Includes a quiet reading zone, group study rooms, digital catalog terminals, and access to NPTEL, Coursera, and other online learning platforms.

10,000+ Books Digital Catalog Study Rooms

Auditorium

A 500-seat fully air-conditioned auditorium equipped with professional AV systems, LED stage lighting, and a high-quality PA system. Hosts guest lectures, cultural events, technical seminars, and annual convocations.

500-Seat Capacity Air Conditioned Pro AV

Cafeteria

A clean, hygienic cafeteria serving freshly prepared meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices. Separate vegetarian and non-vegetarian counters, a juice bar, and air-conditioned seating for 150 students.

Veg & Non-Veg 150 Seats Air Conditioned

Sports Complex

A comprehensive sports complex with both indoor and outdoor facilities. Includes a cricket ground, football field, badminton courts, table tennis, chess zone, and a fully equipped indoor gymnasium — promoting physical health alongside academic excellence.

Outdoor Courts Indoor Games Gymnasium

Wi-Fi Enabled Campus

The entire campus — classrooms, corridors, library, hostels, and open spaces — is covered by high-speed Wi-Fi with 1 Gbps fibre connectivity. Students enjoy uninterrupted internet access for research, online courses, and collaborative projects.

1 Gbps Fibre Full Campus 24/7 Access

Computer Laboratories

Our computer laboratories are the heart of the SBG BCA College experience. Designed to replicate professional development environments, each lab is set up to support everything from introductory programming to advanced AI model training and cloud infrastructure deployment.

More on Campus

Additional Facilities

Beyond the core infrastructure — spaces that enrich campus life.

Smart Classrooms

All classrooms are equipped with digital projectors, interactive whiteboards, and high-quality audio systems for an engaging, multimedia-driven learning experience.

Seminar Halls

Two dedicated seminar halls with 80-seat capacity each — used for guest lectures, workshops, hackathons, and student presentations throughout the year.

Transport Facility

Fleet of college buses covering major areas in and around Belagavi — providing safe, reliable, and affordable transportation for day scholars.

Medical Room

On-campus medical room staffed by a qualified nurse, with a tie-up with a nearby hospital for emergency medical assistance during college hours.

CCTV & Security

The entire campus is monitored by a 24/7 CCTV surveillance system with security personnel stationed at all entry and exit points for student safety.

Printing & Stationery

An on-campus printing and stationery store providing affordable printing, photocopying, binding, and study materials — conveniently located within the campus.
