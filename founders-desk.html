Founder's Desk

A personal message from our Chairman and Founder — his journey, his vision, and his unwavering commitment to transforming education in Karnataka.

Shri. Suresh B. Gowda

Dear Students, Parents & Well-Wishers,

A message from the desk of the Chairman — March 2025

It is with immense pride and deep humility that I welcome you to SBG BCA College — an institution born out of a simple yet powerful belief: that every young person in Karnataka deserves access to world-class technology education, regardless of their background or circumstances.

When I founded SBG Groups over fifteen years ago, I had a single purpose in mind — to create institutions that would stand as pillars of quality, integrity, and ambition in our community. Today, as I look at the hundreds of graduates who have gone on to build successful careers in technology, finance, and entrepreneurship, I feel that purpose being fulfilled, one student at a time.

"I did not build this college for statistics. I built it for the student from a small village who has a dream but no roadmap. We are that roadmap."

The world of technology is evolving at an unprecedented pace. Artificial intelligence, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and data science are no longer niche fields — they are the foundations of every industry. Our BCA program, aligned to Rani Channamma University and continuously refined in partnership with industry, has been built to ensure that our graduates are not merely employable — they are exceptional.

We have invested heavily in modern infrastructure, in hiring faculty who are not just teachers but mentors, and in building placement partnerships with organisations that genuinely value talent. But more than any infrastructure, what I am most proud of is our culture — one of warmth, discipline, and relentless encouragement.

To every prospective student reading this: choose SBG BCA College not because we promise you easy success, but because we commit to walking every step of this journey with you. To every parent who has placed their trust in us — we do not take that trust lightly. It is the single greatest responsibility we carry.

I look forward to welcoming you into our family.

Suresh B. Gowda
Shri. Suresh B. Gowda
Chairman & Founder, SBG BCA College
