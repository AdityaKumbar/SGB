Who We Are

A Legacy of Academic
Excellence & Innovation

SBG BCA College, established under the prestigious SBG Groups, has been a beacon of quality education in Computer Applications for over 15 years. Affiliated to Mumbai University and recognized by the Government of Maharashtra, we offer a comprehensive 3-year BCA program that equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical industry skills.

Our curriculum is continuously updated to reflect the latest trends in technology — from Full-Stack Development to Artificial Intelligence — ensuring our graduates are sought after by top employers nationwide.
